Fast Custom Social Share loads fast, is extensively customizable, and allows you to add social networks yourself.

== Description ==

Very fast, very customizable and very easy to use social share buttons.

Use customizable, static share buttons that work without any external JavaScript. Makes your pages loads faster! Prevent tracking of your users by 3rd parties.

Select from 14 different, professionally designed icon sets! Choose the best set that goes with your theme and your content! Change the style of any set to fit your tastes and your site!

Add any social network which is not already provided! You can activate/deactivate any social network from options.

Easily change the style of the social share to fit with your theme using the in-built what-you-see-is-what-you-get style editor, choose the placement of the share buttons inside your posts, their alignment, customize button appearance, size, margin, turn on/off share text, customize all the features. Create your own style for your social share and use it on your site. Upload your own, different icon for any social network instead of using the default icon!

[youtube https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=PifiXQzKITo]

You can even use different style social shares for different post types! Just assign any different style you want to show for a certain post type to that post type!

Full features:

- Beautifully designed buttons
- Fast - loads no external JavaScript. 
- Customizable - select from ~14 premade styles, create and edit your own!
- Add new social networks yourself!
- Icon sizes from 16px to 64px! Loads the exact icon size for your style and keeps it fast!
- Upload custom icons easily to use in place of default icons!
- Optionally add text to social share buttons
- Optional text for social networks is also customizable - ie you can put 'Tweet' for Twitter and 'Share' for Facebook
- Multi language - translate anything to your own language easily from provided admin panel
- Change display order of the social network buttons easily
- Easily extensible and easy to modify

== Installation ==

1. Activate the plugin
2. The setup wizard will help you choose a style for your site.
3. Done!
